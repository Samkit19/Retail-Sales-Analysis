# Retail Sales Analysis | Excel • SQL Server • Power BI

### Business Intelligence Project using Microsoft Excel, SQL Server & Power BI

This Business Intelligence project analyzes retail sales data using **Microsoft Excel**, **SQL Server**, and **Power BI** to uncover sales trends, evaluate business performance, and support data-driven decision-making through interactive dashboards and business insights.

---

## 🚀 Project Highlights

- 📊 Analyzed **5,000+ retail sales transactions**
- 📈 Designed **interactive Excel & Power BI dashboards**
- 🗄️ Solved **40+ SQL business queries**
- 📑 Prepared a professional business analysis report
- 💡 Generated actionable business insights & recommendations
- ✅ Cross-validated KPIs across Excel, SQL Server & Power BI

---

## 📖 Project Overview

This project demonstrates an end-to-end Business Intelligence workflow—from cleaning raw retail sales data in Microsoft Excel to answering business questions using SQL Server and presenting actionable insights through interactive Power BI dashboards. The objective was to transform raw transactional data into meaningful information that supports better business decisions.

## 🎯 Business Problem

Retail businesses generate thousands of daily transactions, making it difficult to identify meaningful trends using raw data alone.

This project transforms raw retail sales data into interactive dashboards and business insights that help evaluate business performance, identify trends, monitor KPIs, and support data-driven decision-making.

## 💡 Key Business Insights

- Grocery generated the highest overall sales.
- Fashion delivered the highest overall profit.
- Maharashtra recorded the highest state-wise sales.
- Mumbai generated the highest city-wise revenue.
- UPI emerged as the most preferred payment method.
- October and November recorded peak sales performance.
- Fashion achieved the highest overall profit margin.

---

## 📈 Business Recommendations

- Increase inventory for high-performing product categories.
- Focus marketing campaigns on top-performing regions.
- Strengthen inventory planning before seasonal demand peaks.
- Encourage digital payment adoption through promotional offers.
- Review pricing strategies for low-margin products.
- Improve customer retention through targeted loyalty initiatives.

---

## ⚠️ Challenges Faced

- Validated KPI consistency across Excel, SQL Server, and Power BI.
- Resolved minor differences caused by decimal values being displayed as whole numbers.
- Balanced dashboard design to present detailed insights without clutter.
- Structured SQL queries to efficiently answer multiple business questions.
